# New Dying State 

* You remain conscious at 0 HP.
* You die when your HP reaches *−½ your maximum HP*.
* If your HP becomes positive, you immediately stabilize.
* Stabilise action has been removed
* You no longer make a death saving throw at the start of each turn.
* You can still take actions on your turn.
* *Movement:* If you use a move action to move *more than half your speed*, make a death saving throw.
* *Standard action:* When you take a standard action, make a death saving throw.
* *Bonus actions* do *not* require a death saving throw.
* *On a failed death save:* You take *1d6 damage for each death save you’ve failed since your last long rest*.
